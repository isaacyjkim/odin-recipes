Roasted Potatoes

This is a simple, easy to make roasted potatoes recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the potato wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Roast for 30-35 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
