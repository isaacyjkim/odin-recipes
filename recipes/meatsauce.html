Tomato Meat Sauce

This is a simple, easy to make tomato based meat sauce. The secret to this sauce is using delicious, fresh ingredients, and letting time do the work for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 half onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of RAO'S tomato saucce
  • 1 half of a small carrot

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the RAO'S tomato sauce and grated carrot.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
